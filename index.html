Medical image registration

Image registration is an optimization problem with the goal of finding the perfect allignment of two or more images of different sensors or capture times.

  • In this research project a dataset of medical CT scans is used.
  • Evolutionary Strategies is used in an iterative process for finding the optimal alignment of each image pair.
  • Part of research intern at Computational Intelligence Lab of Prof. Chuan-Kang Ting at the National Chung Cheng University Taiwan

Droplet particle analysis of spray images

Analysing spray image of dropelts. Images are taken by camera connected to rasperry pie. The following image processing pipeline ist used for extracting the sizes of droplets for the different DV values.

  • image denoising
  • watershed algorithm for instance segmentation
  • finding minimum enclosing circle for each object
  • calculating size of objects with the measured field of view fo the camera (dpi)
